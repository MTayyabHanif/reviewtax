About Us

For any suggestions, or if you want your company review, please email admin@reviews.tax today.

Reviews.Tax is a free online resource that strives to provide both business and individual taxpayers with company comparisons and free content. Companies cannot pay for a better position as part of our service. However, we may get compensated by a company when you call or request a quote from our website.

As former tax company owners, we use a proprietary algorithm in order to rank our top ten list using factors such as diversity of tax services, average case fees, BBB ratings, customer reviews, years in business, professionals on staff, refund policies, and more.

One of the founders of reviews.tax is a tax professional and both founders use to own a tax relief company. Our goal is to provide information so you can make an informed decision.

Our Partners: W Tax Group, Tax Defense Network, Community Tax, Justice Tax, Tax Resource Group

Reviews.Tax was created as a way to cut through all the marketing and fluff surrounding tax relief companies online. There are many companies that provide tax problem resolution out there so we decided to leverage our experience to help taxpayers make an educated decision.

As former tax firm owners, we understand the tax relief industry. We leveraged our deep experience to craft an algorithm that leverages many factors in ranking tax relief firms.
